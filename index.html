How the AVENA Deal Score Works

Each property is scored 0–100 based on multiple weighted factors:


Price vs Market (40%): We compare the property's €/m² against the area average for comparable new builds. Properties priced 20%+ below market get maximum points.


Off-Plan Potential (20%): Properties with completion dates 12+ months out in early sales phases get bonus points. Developers need pre-sales to secure bank financing, so early buyers often get 10-25% below eventual market price.


Value Density (15%): For villas: plot size relative to price. For apartments: built m² relative to price. Larger area per euro = higher score.


Location Premium (15%): Beach proximity, golf access, infrastructure quality, and area growth trajectory.


Developer Track Record (10%): Years in market, delivery history, reputation. Established developers with 20+ year records score highest.


Pro Tip: The best deals are typically off-plan projects 12-24 months from completion, from established developers in areas showing strong YoY growth but still below €2,500/m². Triple benefit: early-bird pricing, capital appreciation during construction, and below-market entry.
